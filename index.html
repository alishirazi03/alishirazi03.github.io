Background

About Me

I am a Biosciences graduate from COMSATS University Islamabad, where my academic journey transitioned from traditional wet-lab techniques — including DNA extraction and PCR — to the high-impact world of Bioinformatics and Computational Biology.

My undergraduate research focused on the Genetic Basis of Gallstone Disease, specifically analyzing the role of the ATP8B1 gene. This project deepened my skills in genome sequence analysis and reinforced my commitment to using computational tools to solve biological mysteries. I am particularly interested in how Artificial Intelligence (AI) and modern data analytics can accelerate discoveries in genomics and molecular biology.

Having experience in both wet-lab DNA analysis and bioinformatic pipelines, I aim to solve complex biological problems through an integrative approach. I am currently preparing to pursue a Master's degree to further specialize in Bioinformatics and contribute to global research efforts.

Outside research, I enjoy scientific communication and have a track record in scientific presentations and collaborative group research. I am proficient in Python and R for biological data analysis, data visualization, and specialized bioinformatic software pipelines.
